HYPERADRENO CORTICISIM (Cushings Syndrome)

TYSON

A DACHSHUND STORY

DISCOVERY

This is Tyson my 9 year old Dachshund. In May 2019 his health and general well being started to slowly deteriorate, he appeared to be in a constant state of discomfort and tiredness. He was swollen around his stomach and was also tender there. His energy levels were low to the point he didn't want to go for a walk or play with a ball. We then noticed he was losing hair from his tail and was drinking more he also seemed to be constantly hungry. Then followed the first of many trips to the local vet. After a series of vitimans intovenus fluids and blood tests the decision was made to send him to the University hospital for small animals to conduct further tests.

SYMPTOMS INCLUDE:

  • Hair Loss
  • Swollen Belly
  • Thinning Skin
  • Increased Appetite
  • Increased Thirst
  • Lethargic
