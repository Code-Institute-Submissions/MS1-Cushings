TREATMENT

If a tumor is found in the Pituitary gland then medication would be used to supress the production of cortisol as it's inoperable due to it's location.
If an Adrenal tumor is diagnosed then medication would be prescribed to shrink thetumor followed by surgery to remove it. However treatment for Cushings is not always recommended as the medication can be very invasive and bring it's own problems.
So in mild cases or when not showing many of the syptoms regular exercise and a low fat diet given in small regular portions can be helpful as to not overwork the Liver.
